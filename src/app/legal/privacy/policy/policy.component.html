Privacy Policy

Engage’s Privacy Policy describes how Engage collects, uses, and shares your personal data.

Please take a moment to familiarize yourself with our privacy practices, accessible below, and contact us if you have any questions.

What is Categorized as Personal Data?

Engage treats any data that relates to an identified or identifiable individual, or that is linked or linkable to them by Engage, as “personal data”, regardless of where the individual is located geographically. This means that data that can directly identify - such as your name - is personal data. Data that does not directly identify you, but that could identify you, such as any stored address, is also treated as personal data.

This Privacy Policy covers how Engage or an Engage-affiliated company handles personal data, whether you interact with us on our websites, through Engage apps or services, or in person (including by phone or video call). Engage may also link to third-parties on our services. Engage’s Privacy Policy does not apply to how third-parties define personal data or how they use it. We encourage you to read their privacy policies and know your privacy rights before making any interactions with them.

Your Privacy Rights

At Engage, we respect your ability to know, access, correct, transfer, restrict the processing of, and delete your personal data. We have provided these rights to our global user base and if you choose to exercise these privacy rights, you have the right not to be treated in a discriminatory way nor to receive a lesser degree of service from Engage. Engage does not sell your data, including as “sale” is defined in British Columbia, Canada.

To exercise your privacy rights and choices, please contact us at support@engageapp.net.

There may be situations where Engage will be unable to grant your request. For example, if you ask us to remove any data where Engage is legally obligated to maintain a record to comply with law. We may also decline to grant a request where doing so would undermine our legitimate use of your data for anti-fraud and security purposes, such as when you request the deletion of an account that is currently being investigated for fraud or security concerns. Other reasons your request may be denied are if it jeopardizes the privacy of others, is frivolous or vexatious, or would be extremely impractical for Engage to carry out.

Personal Data Engage Collects from You

At Engage, we believe that great services and privacy aren’t exclusive from each other. This means that we strive to collect only the necessary personal data that we need for our services to function. The personal data that Engage collects depends on how you interact with Engage.

When you create an Engage account, use our services, contact us (including by social media), participate in online surveys, or otherwise interact with Engage, we may collect a variety of information, including:

  • Account Information

    Your Engage account and related account details, including your name, email address, account status, and age.

  • Contact Information

    Data such as name, email address, phone number, or other contact information.

  • Usage Data

    Data about your activity on our services, including the amount you use our services.

  • School ID Data

    In some cases, we may ask for a school-issued ID to verify the identity of our tutors, and ensure that tutors who perform below our standards and are terminated are unable to create new accounts with false identities.

  • Other Information You Provide to Engage

    Details such as the content of your communications with Engage, including interactions with our public relations team, our support team, and any contact through social media channels.

You are not required to provide the personal data that we request. However, if you choose not to do so, we will be unable to provide our services to you in many cases.

Engage’s Use of Personal Data

Engage uses personal data to power our services, to communicate with you, for security, for fraud prevention, and to comply with law. We may also use personal data for other purposes with your consent.

Engage will only use your personal data when we have a valid legal basis to do so. Depending on the circumstances, Engage may rely on your consent or the fact that processing is necessary to fulfill a contract with you, protect your vital interests or those of other persons, or to comply with law. We may also process your personal data where we believe it is in our or others’ legitimate interests, taking into consideration your interests, rights, and expectations. If you have any questions about the legal basis, you can contact support@engageapp.net.

  • Power Our Services

    Engage collects personal data necessary to power our services, which may include personal data collected to personalize or improve our offerings, for internal purposes such as auditing or data analysis, or for troubleshooting. For example, when you select a tutor through the Engage Network, we collect data on tutors that you choose in order to provide you with the requested tutor, as well as to track the tutor’s volunteer hours.

  • Communicate with You

    To respond to communications, reach out to you about your account, provide other relevant information, or request information or feedback. At times, we may use your personal data to send important notices, such as communications about activities on your account or about changes to our terms, conditions, or policies.

  • Security and Fraud Prevention

    To protect individuals, employees, and Engage and for loss prevention and to prevent fraud, including to protect individuals, employees, and Engage for the benefit of all our users, and pre-screening or scanning uploaded content for potentially illegal content, including, but not limited to, material that violates copyright or intellectual property laws (commonly known as "pirated" material).

  • Comply with Law

    To comply with applicable law - for example to satisfy tax or reporting obligations, or to comply with a lawful governmental request.

Engage does not use algorithms or profiling to make any decisions that would significantly affect you without the opportunity for human review.

Engage retains personal data only for so long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, including as described in this Privacy Policy, or as required by law. We will retain your personal data for the period necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy and our service-specific privacy summaries. When assessing retention periods, we first carefully examine whether it is necessary to retain the personal data collected from you, and if retention is required to work, work to retain the personal data for the shortest possible period permissible under law.

Protection of Personal Data at Engage

At Engage, we believe that the highest standard of privacy relies on the highest standard of security. We use administrative, technical, and software safeguards to protect your personal data, taking into account the nature of the personal data and the processing, as well as the threats posed to your personal data. We are constantly working to improve on these safeguards to help keep your personal data secure.

Personal Data and Children

Engage understands the importance of safeguarding the personal data of children, which we consider to be any individuals under the age of 13 or equivalent age as specified by law in your jurisdiction. That is why Engage has implemented additional processes and protections to help keep children’s personal data safe.

To access Engage services, a child must have an Engage Network account. A child Engage Network account must be created by their parent or guardian, and the parents or guardian must give explicit consent for the child to have an account. Parents will give their child consent to use Engage services through a three-step process that is outlined in the Engage Network Terms of Service.

If we learn that a child’s personal data was collected without appropriate authorization, it will be deleted as soon as possible.

Cookies and Other Technologies

Engage’s websites, online services, interactive applications, email messages, and advertisements may use “cookies” or other technologies. These technologies help us to better understand user behaviour including for security and fraud prevention purposes, tell us which parts of our websites people have visited, and facilitate and measure the effectiveness of advertisements and web searches. Our use of cookies is outlined in the Engage Network Cookies Policy.

If you prefer that Engage does not use cookies, you are able to disable them on your device. Please consult the applicable documentation for your specific web browser for instructions.

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

To ensure that your personal data is secure, we communicate our privacy and security guidelines to Engage employees and strictly enforce any and all privacy safeguards within the company.
