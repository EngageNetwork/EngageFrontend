About Us

Engage Network was started with the goal of connecting prospective students with vetted tutors in high-school through university, via our in-house online software. The co-founders recognized the void of free education enrichment for students with aspirations restricted by finances, and similarly the lack of volunteer opportunities for high school students as a result of the COVID-19 pandemic, and aimed to find a solution.
